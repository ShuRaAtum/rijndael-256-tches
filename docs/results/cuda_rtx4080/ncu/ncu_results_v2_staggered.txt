Rijndael-256 ECB Nsight Compute Profiling Results — Staggered Store Patch
==========================================================================
Device: NVIDIA AD103 (CC 8.9, RTX 4080)
Workload: 1M blocks (32 MiB), 256-bit key
Date: 2026-04-09
Patch: Te0_s/Td0_s init loop uses staggered column index
       Te0_s[i][(b + warpThreadIndex) & 31] = te0_val;

Note: Post-warmup launches used (indices 2-5 for tpb=128, 8-11 for tpb=256).

============================================================
8. Key Findings
============================================================
1. Staggered store eliminates 99.4-99.6% of V3 bank conflicts.
   Total conflicts dropped from ~65.5M to ~290K-389K.

2. V3 kernel speed improved 1.51-1.59x at tpb=128 (the primary config).
   Cycles: 1.72M → 1.08M (encrypt), 1.70M → 1.12M (decrypt).

3. V3 Encrypt records fewer SM cycles than V2 Encrypt at tpb=128
   (0.97x cycles).

4. V3's IPC rises from 52.5 to 87.9 at tpb=128, vs V2's 59.5.

5. V3's low occupancy (15.6%) does not prevent it from matching or
   exceeding V2 throughput at tpb=128 once bank conflicts are resolved.
